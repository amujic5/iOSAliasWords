Alias Words is an absolute hit for all ages! If you claimed for yourself that you are resourceful, skilful and sharp on the tongue, this is your chance to prove it. It is ideal game for a friendly and social gatherings and therefore have an extra motivation for proving. In this game you have to describe a given word using its synonyms, but you may not use given word, nor the root of it. The base has over 3500 terms, and are available in the following languages: English, Croatian, German, French, Swedish, Slovenian, Italian, Spanish. Questions, compliments, suggestions leave in the comments.
